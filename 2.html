Dawn Prime Academy

Dawn Prime Academy is a mixed day and boarding school in Iganga, Uganda
Dawn Prime Academy is also known for consistently being one of the top-performing primary schools in the Iganga district
Dawn Prime Academy is a mixed day and boarding school in Iganga, Uganda
Dawn Prime Academy was established in 1995
Dawn Prime Academy was established by Samuel Mpaabe and his wife Nakalembe
Dawn Prime Academy has a main "parent school" with around 500 pupils and 32 teachers, and a "sister school" with about 150 students and 14 teachers
Dawn Prime Academy also emphasizes co-curricular activities like chess, table tennis, volleyball, netball, and football
